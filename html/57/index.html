About

Nosson's Schwarma has some of the best food in town. We have a wide assortment of dishes, ranging from salads and dips to falafel, soups, and of course... schwarma.

We're conveniently located in the center of town, and have a spacious parking lot to accomodate all our customers. We are also proud to have the hashgacha of the בד"ץ עדה חרדית and we're also careful to comply with all the health standards of the משרד הבריאות. We're passionate about giving our customers the great culinary experience they deserve and look forward to serving youin our restaurant! Click here to see our delicious menu!
